About IMPACT COLLEGE

The Institute of Management and Planning & Advanced Computer Training (IMPACT) college, situated in Patna District of Bihar that was set up in 2014. Affiliated under Aryabhatta Knowledge University (AKU), Patna. IMPACT college offers 4 Undergraduate courses in the domains of mass communication, commerce, computer applications, and business administration. The college offers direct admission to the students based on the merit list. The admission form can be accessed online on the college website.
